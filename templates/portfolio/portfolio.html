The blog

This is a portfolio I made using python, django, and tailwind css for the styling, since I'm not the best designer I figured I could use something like tailwind, also i always wanted to tried it.

This site has a home, and about page and a contact page, all of them are static pages, the blog hoewer, is a full dynamic page, I have a dashboard where I can create, edit and delete the posts, plus you can see the list of posts and any individual post you select, each post also has a category, with it's icon and the usual stuff. You can also filter the posts by category (although usually they are filter by date).

Dentist Dashboard

This is a dashboard I created with Python, Django and Bootstrap. It is for a dentist office, and it's function is to keep a record of the patients.

There is a form that the patient has to fill, and that's about it for the patient, but for the dentist there is a list of the patients, the detail of the patients and several studies he/she can create, the dentist can also do anotations and keep a record of the payments of the patient
